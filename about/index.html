Our Story

Built on Hard Work & Honesty

Spiros Aluminum & Roofing Inc. was founded in 1986 by Spiros Koutsoulianos in Richmond Hill, Ontario. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of the GTA's most trusted roofing contractors — with a team of over 50 skilled professionals.

Over nearly four decades, we've completed more than 10,000 installations across the Greater Toronto Area, earning a reputation built on quality craftsmanship, reliable service, and genuine care for every customer.

We are 100% Canadian — locally owned, locally operated, and proudly serving our community. When you call Spiros, you're not calling a call centre or a franchise. You're talking directly to people who live and work in the same communities you do.

Mr. Spiros himself remains involved in the business to this day, ensuring that every project meets the same high standard he established when he first picked up a nail gun in 1986.
